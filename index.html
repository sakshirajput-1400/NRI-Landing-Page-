With India at the Center

Living outside India often brings professional opportunity and global exposure. At the same time, it creates financial complexity that is easy to underestimate.

Assets remain in India. Family responsibilities continue. Regulations and execution differ across countries. Currency exposure adds another layer of decision making.

This page serves as an overview of how we work with NRIs and global Indians, and helps you navigate to advisory considerations specific to your region.
